Under the Hood

How It Works

The science and technology behind IrisAI

01
📊

Data Collection

The famous Fisher Iris dataset contains 150 samples across three species. Each sample has four measurements: sepal length, sepal width, petal length, and petal width.

02
⚙️

Preprocessing

Species labels are encoded numerically using scikit-learn's LabelEncoder. Data is split 80/20 into training and test sets with a fixed random seed for reproducibility.

03
🌲

Random Forest

An ensemble of decision trees votes on the species. Each tree is trained on a random subset of data, and the majority vote becomes the final prediction — robust to noise.

04
🎯

Prediction

Your four measurements are passed to the trained model. The predicted numeric label is decoded back to the species name and displayed instantly.
